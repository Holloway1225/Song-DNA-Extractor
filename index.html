CONCEPT EXTRACTOR

Paste lyrics. Get one vivid usable concept for your lyric generator.

What this does
This tool reads an existing song, figures out what it is actually about, then turns that meaning into one fresh generator-ready concept. It does not quote the song, copy the hook, or force a preset mood. The output should be vivid, accurate, and ready to paste into your lyric generator.

Best use: paste lyrics you like, extract the emotional structure, then generate a new song from the concept instead of copying the original.
